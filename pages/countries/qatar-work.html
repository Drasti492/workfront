Work in Qatar

Manual labor jobs open to foreigners (all genders, low to mid-skill). Requirements: Age 18+, work visa (e.g., employer-sponsored), basic English/Arabic, health check. Salaries in Qatari Riyal (QAR), tax-free, often include accommodation and food allowances (QR500 for housing, QR300 for food if not provided). Apply for any below.

Assembly and production work in factories. Req: Physically fit, 18+. Salary: QAR 40k-55k (approx. QR3,300-4,600/month).
