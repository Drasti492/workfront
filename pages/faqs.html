Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any fee to apply?
No hidden fees. Only official processing costs apply.
How long does the process take?
Typically 4-8 weeks, depending on the country and path and applicant profile.
What documents do I need?
Passport, education certificates, and proof of funds/language skills, check on the application-form.
